Brides of the Lizard God
(2013+)

James plays guitar and sings in Brides of the Lizard God, purveyors of arenaless arena rock about dragons and feelings. Graduates of Seattle bands ranging from lo-fi to metalcore, they operate under a single guiding principle: that no idea is too ridiculous, provided it is also awesome.

The Brides are:

James Sutter (guitar/vox)
Patrick Tewson (guitar/vox)
Andy Kreek (bass/vox)
Tim Wellman (bass/vox, emeritus)
Eric Baker (drums)
