Shadow at Morning
(2006-2010)

James played bass and sang in the hardcore metal band Shadow at Morning from 2006 to 2010, achieving his lifelong dream of headlining Seattle's Showbox, getting songs spun on several metal-friendly radio stations in the Seattle area, and opening for metal legends like 3 Inches of Blood.

Shadow at Morning was:

James Sutter (bass/vox)
Ryan Zielonka (guitar)
Ryan McMichael (vox)
Eric Baker (drums)
