Nonfiction

During college and immediately thereafter, James worked as a freelance reporter and music critic for The University of Washington Daily, The Stranger, The Seattle Times, and other local newspapers. Once he began writing for Dragon and Dungeon magazines, he decided reporting wasn't nearly as interesting as making stuff up, and abandoned all journalistic aspirations. While most of those early articles have thankfully been lost to the aether, some of his more recent essays and editorials are collected below.
