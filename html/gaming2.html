Gaming

James is the co-creator of the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game campaign setting, and works as the Senior Editor/Fiction Editor for Paizo Publishing. In his role as a key developer and editor, he has helped shape the world of the Pathfinder RPG since its inception, working on hundreds of products--listed below are only those supplements that James personally wrote. In addition, James worked as an Assistant Editor for Dungeon magazine.
