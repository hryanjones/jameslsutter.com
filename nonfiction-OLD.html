Nonfiction

During college and immediately thereafter, James worked as a freelance reporter for The University of Washington Daily, The Stranger, The Seattle Times, and other newspapers. Once he discovered Paizo Publishing, he decided reporting wasn't nearly as interesting as making stuff up, and abandoned legitimate journalism entirely, save for the occasional guest blog or essay.
