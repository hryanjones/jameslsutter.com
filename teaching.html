James loves teaching about both the artistic and professional sides of a career in speculative fiction and game design. With extensive experience as an acquiring editor, author, game designer, and creative director, James has taught classes on everything from fantasy worldbuilding and designing better monsters to breaking into the publishing industry and making your manuscript stand out from the slush pile. He's been an instructor, guest speaker, or panelist at dozens of literary and gaming events and workshops, including the Clarion West Writer's Workshop, Digipen Institute of Technology, the Gen Con Writer's Symposium, Emerald City Comicon, ICON South Africa, and more.

To book James at your event, or for private coaching and editorial services, email jameslsutter@gmail.com.

Upcoming Classes & Appearances

Clarion West One-Day Workshop: Worldbuilding Made Easy

March 28, 2020 — Seattle, WA

In this day-long workshop for Clarion West, James will present a number of tried-and-true tactics for tricking yourself into developing intriguing science fiction and fantasy worlds in just minutes, even when you think you’re out of ideas. Learn how to ask yourself the right questions to jumpstart your imagination, letting you quickly create compelling cultures, creatures, religions, magic systems, and more. We’ll also cover how to avoid common worldbuilding traps, and how to use these same principles to surprise yourself with stories you didn’t expect. Finally, you’ll put these techniques to use in guided exercises, coming up with fun new setting ideas on the spot. For information, visit https://www.clarionwest.org/workshops/oneday/james-l-sutter/.
