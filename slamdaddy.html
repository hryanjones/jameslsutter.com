slamDaddy
(2000-2003)

From 2000 to 2003, James was the lead singer and rhythm guitarist for the teenage punk band slamDaddy. (It was a strange era for band names.) The group self-released two EPs, which were positively reviewed on KEXP and in The Stranger.

slamDaddy was:

James Sutter (guitar/vox)
Ryan Zielonka (guitar)
Andy Kreek (bass)
Megan Edwards (drums)
