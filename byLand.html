By Land or By Sea
(2007-2009)

Started as an acoustic side project during Shadow at Morning, By Land or By Sea bounced from folk and indie to screamo and hardcore, featuring appearances by Seattle musicians like drummer Eric Baker, Sarah Shay of The Mongrel Jews (pictured), and cellist Kaia Chessen.
